Current Projects (github)

Company Queue

Students come to the Texas A&M Career Fair with limited time, as they have to come between classes. It is unfortunate when students have to wait in long lines to talk to a single company. I am in the process of creating a system that will have students join a queue, and text them whenever the recruiters have space.

Past Projects

Company Selection

The Career Fair chairs spend hours deciding on which companies will come to the career fair, which is based upon a rubric. I automated this process using python, javascript, html, and css, so that I can advise them on which companies should come. I also organize the data neatly based off majors.

Backpack Check-In/Out

Having backpacks at the career fair is not only hazardous but also tiring. For that reason, we needed a system to store backpacks for when students attend. Using javascript, html, and css, I created a system that allows students to type in their UIN or swipe their student ID, and it will give them a location to put their backback. They can follow the same steps to retrieve it.

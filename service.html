The Citizens Foundation

The Citizens Foundation is a nonprofit that supposrts the eduation of underprivileged children in Pakistan. Each year, they host one big fundraiser in Austin, and we usually accumulate over $200k each year. My role these past few years has been to raise dues and collect payments. I have been emailing friends and family, and donating lots of my own money. This past summer, I actually got to visit Pakistan, and I got to see the schools that were being made and met the children that were able to go to school because of us.
